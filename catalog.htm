About
Somerset Township Library is a small independent public library that has been in existence for over 50 years. Somerset Township Library serves the residents of Somerset Township along with the residents of the surround areas such as Mirror Lake and Moscow Township. Although Somerset Township Library receives no federal or state funding, the book selection is updated by purchasing best sellers each month. Funds received through fines, donations and book sales are used for purchasing children, youth and adult books.
