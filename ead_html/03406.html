Biographical/Historical note

Shirley Harbin was born on December 26, 1931 in Santa Cruz, California. She received her Ph.D. in Theatre and Recreation from Wayne State University in 1977. She worked for the Detroit Recreation Department (DRD) from 1963-1991. As Performing Arts Director for the DRD, Nis Harbin directed 10 regional festivals, 3 world festivals involving more than 20 countries, wrote and consulted on grants, and edited and published newsletters, directories, and handbooks. In addition, she created cooperative drama and music programs serving more than 1,000 youth between 1973 and 1991. Ms Harbin was also actively involved with the Wayne State University Theatre, both as instructor and supporter.
