Biographical/Historical note

Journalist for Detroit Journal and the Detroit Times.Born in old Corktown in Detroit on May 20, 1689. Veteran reporter who started his career with the Detroit News. Also worked on papers in Flint and Milwaukee and as the dramatic editor on the Detroit Journal. In 1921 he moved to the Detroit Times as their music and drama critic and remained with this paper until his death on February 17, 1939. Mr. Holmes wrote many plays in his spare time but none were ever performed. He was married to Clara O'Dea in 1909 and they had two children, Robert and Beverly. Detroit News, Feb. 17, 1939 Trend 0' Times, Mar. 1930 (from Detroit New. Feb. 17, 1939; Trent O' Times, Mar. 1930)
