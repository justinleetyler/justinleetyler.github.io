General Physical Description note
(34 boxes, 6 volumes, 1 large manuscript).
Abstract
Primarily board of director meeting minutes, finance reports, committee materials and publicity. Also includes biographies, photographs and autographs of prominent people who were speakers, such as Dr. Joyce Brothers, the Reverend Jesse Jackson, and local politicians.

Scope and Contents note

The numerous committees cover all aspects of club functions. This series contains committee reports and materials. Separate sub-series are devoted to the large amount of material relating to Finance, Membership, Programs, and Publicity. Photographs are all contained together, as part of the Publicity/Public Relations section.
