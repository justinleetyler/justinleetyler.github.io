Biographical/Historical note

The first generation of the Hodgman family begins with the brothers Francis Hodgman (1839-1907) and Charles E. Hodgman (1844-1923), it continues on in the second generation with Harry Hodgman (1871-1949) and his wife, Alberta V. Stewart (18671922) and then the collection ends in the third generation with Flora Elizabeth Hodgman (1900-1993) and her husband, Wallace A. Temple (1906-1971).

Return to Table of Contents »

Scope and Contents note

Correspondence and photographs of three generations of the Hodgman family, early settlers of Kalamazoo county, Mich. Collection includes extensive wartime correspondence from the Southwest Pacific, and a small set of letters from Canadian Missionaries in China during the 1920's.
