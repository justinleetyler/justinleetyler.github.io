Biographical/Historical note

Surveyor general of the Northwest, U.S. Commissioner of the General Land Office, Governor of Ohio and U.S. Senator.Born in Carlisle, England, Jun 19, 1766; studied medicine in Univ. of Penn. and grad. 1789; Methodist deacon, Nov. 19, 1792; moved to Chillicothe, Ohio, 1796; preached & practiced; 1799 chosen to legislator of N.W. territory; 1802 president of convent. forming constitut. of Ohio; 1803 first governor of Ohio; reelected 1805; Dec. 1807 U.S. senator; resigned Mar. 3, 1809; 1812 commissioner of general land office; 1814 surveyor gener. of publ. lands N.W. Ohio river; died in this office, in Chillicothe, Ohio, Aug, 9, 1829. (from Appleton's Cyclop. of Americ. Biograph.) (blue index cards)
