Abstract
This genealogy collection contains materials owned by Paul H. Townsend. The families are primarily the Townsend, Hand and Holmes families, but there are many other included. See Box 1:1 for a complete list of family names. Two volumes include letters from H.C. Campion Jr., concerning the Townsend genealogy, 1933-1945. The rest of the volumes contain genealogy material on families from A-Y. The earliest it in the collection is a letter believed to be dated 1915.
