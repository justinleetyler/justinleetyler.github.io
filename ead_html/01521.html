Abstract
Personal and business papers reflecting his career and interests as County Clerk, County Judge, receiver of Bank of Tecumseh, Mich., and fire insurance company official. Family genealogy.

Biographical/Historical note

Born in Woodstock, Vt. Jan. 25,1807; educ. in schools in Woodstock; moved to Harrisburg, Pa., 1825; then to Tecumseh, Mich., 1832; delegate to Clay convent., Wash.,1832; teacher first grammar school, 1832-36; merchant, 1836-39; second clerk of Lenawee Co., 1834-37; first clerk, 1837-40(?); Co. Judge, 1840; assignee of Bank of Tecumseh, 1942 died in Tecumseh, June 18,1884. (from Descendants of Francis Le Baron,p.77 - Hist.& Biogr. of Lenawee Co. ,Mich. , v.1. p.114 ) (blue index cards)
