Biographical/Historical note

Three-term Mayor of Albany, N.Y., merchant, banker, who was interested in the settlement and development of Michigan.An iron founder, merchant, banker, and mayor for three terms of Albany, N.Y. He was interested in the settlement and development of Michigan, one of his agents being Shubael Conant. (from Reynolds, C. Albany Chronicles. 1906. p.474; Ms/Townsend, John papers) (blue index cards)
