Title: Detroit Study Club records,
Dates: 1898-2008
Quantity: 5.5 linear ft. (11 boxes, 2 volumes)
Abstract: Correspondence, constitution, history, minutes, record books, reports, yearbooks, scrapbook, and photos, relating to the organization and its civic interests.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

African American women's organization; established 1898.
